Sewing Machine.

A sewing machine is used to stich fabric and other matierals together with thread.

01 Novice
-You are already a novice. Congrats!
02 Competent
-Know the parts of the sewing machine and their functions.

 -How to fill and install/threat the bobbin.

 -How to install/thread the upper thread.

 -Sewing in a straight line with a straight stitch.

 -Reinforcement stiches (back-stitching at start and end of sewing line).
03 Proficient
-How to sew a curve with a straight stitch.

 -How to change stitch length and width.

 -How to change needle.

 -How to make the printer heat.

 -How to sew around a corner.
04 Advanced
-Using other stitches, especially the zig-zag stitch and button hold.

 -Cleaning and oiling the machine/shuttle race.

 -Changing the thread tension to fix issues with the thread, while sewing.
05 Expert
-Design and create a project that improves the MakerSpace.
