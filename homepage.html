Levels.

All tools have the same ranking scheme, but different requirements for each tool. To view general guidelines click the rank.

Novice Requirements:

  • Supervised on all tools.

Competent Requirements:

  • May use tools unsupervised.

Proficient Requirements:

  • May supervise others.

Advanced Requirements:

  • Produce something that is beneficial to the MakerSpace.

Expert Requirements:

  • Required clean and maintain tools on Wednesdays and available to certify others (Confirmation period first tri)
